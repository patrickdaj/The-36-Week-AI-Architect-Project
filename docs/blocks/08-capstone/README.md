# 🎓 Unit 8 — Capstone

<small>Weeks 33–36 · 2 blocks</small>

[→ Start Block 33–34](33-34-design-and-build/index.md)

---

Everything converges here.

You'll design and build an AI-powered security operations platform — your portfolio centerpiece and the proof that you've gone from "person who uses Copilot" to "person who architects AI systems." This isn't a tutorial project. It's a system you design from scratch based on the patterns, tools, and hard-won lessons from the previous 32 weeks.

The capstone has two phases: Design & Build (weeks 33–34) and Ship & Present (weeks 35–36). You'll write architecture docs, build the system, create deployment guides, and prepare a presentation that demonstrates architect-level thinking.

---

## What You'll Build

- **AI-Powered Security Operations Platform** — your design, your architecture decisions
  - Threat intelligence pipeline with AI analysis
  - Intelligent alert triage
  - Agent-based automated investigation
  - RAG knowledge management
  - Compliance monitoring
- Full **architecture documentation** — system diagrams, ADRs, threat model, deployment guide
- A **presentation or blog series** based on your 36-week journey

---

## Specialization Tracks

After the capstone, pick one to go deeper:

1. **AI Security Architect** — Red-teaming LLMs, AI governance frameworks, securing AI pipelines
2. **AI Platform Architect** — Infrastructure, model serving, scaling, multi-cloud AI deployment
3. **AI Application Architect** — Complex agent systems, multi-model orchestration, domain-specific AI
